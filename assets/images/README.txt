IMAGE PLACEMENT GUIDE
=====================

Please add your three photos to this folder:

1. myphoto1.jpg - Hero section (blob-shaped portrait)
   Recommended: 800x800px or larger, square format

2. myphoto2.jpg - Featured work section (background overlay)
   Recommended: 1920x1080px or larger, landscape format

3. myphoto3.jpg - About me section (rounded rectangle)
   Recommended: 600x800px or larger, portrait format

Once you add these images with the exact names above, they will automatically appear on your portfolio website!
